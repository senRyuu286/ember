A completely free, minimalist fitness tracker designed to build consistent workout habits. Ember strips away the bloat, paywalls, and complex social feeds found in modern apps, helping you focus entirely on the "slow burn" of daily progress.

> **Note:** Ember utilizes `Supabase` to securely sync your data to the cloud. Check out the project documentation and Privacy Policy here.

## Features

- **Distraction-Free Logging:** Quickly log exercises, sets, reps, and weights without navigating through cluttered or confusing menus.

- **Streak Tracking:** A visual "Ember" tracker that rewards consistency, encouraging you to keep your daily fire lit and your momentum going.

- **Cross-Platform Architecture:** Built natively for both Android and iOS devices from a unified codebase, ensuring a buttery-smooth experience.

- **Cloud Sync:** Seamlessly backs up your workout history and authentication securely using Supabase.
